# I. BRANDS

### 1. Bandai (Bandai Namco)

- **Type:** Official Model Manufacturer
- **Description:** The sole owner of the official license to produce "Gunpla" (Gundam Plastic Model). Bandai is renowned for its multi-colored molding technology, snap-fit assembly (no glue required), and a diverse grading system from High Grade (HG) to Perfect Grade (PG). This is the gold standard of the robot model industry.

### 2. Motor Nuclear

- **Type:** Third-party Model Manufacturer
- **Description:** A famous Chinese brand specializing in mecha kits with stylized, aggressive designs. Their kits often feature die-cast frames for a solid, heavy feel and include sharp, distinct effect parts, offering a completely different experience from Bandai's traditional style.

### 3. GodHand

- **Type:** Tools
- **Description:** A legendary Japanese tool brand, famous for the "Ultimate Nipper" (SPN-120). GodHand nippers feature a razor-thin, single-edged blade that cuts through plastic smoothly, leaving almost no nub marks and significantly reducing sanding time.

### 4. Mr. Hobby (GSI Creos)

- **Type:** Paints & Supplies
- **Description:** The brand offering the most complete ecosystem of paints and chemicals for Gunpla. Notable products include "Gundam Marker" for easy panel lining and painting, the "Mr. Color" paint line, and various Topcoat sprays to adjust the model's final finish (matte/gloss).

### 5. Dspiae

- **Type:** Tools
- **Description:** A rising tool brand favored for its modern design, high quality, and competitive pricing. Dspiae is known for single-edged nippers, advanced glass files for polishing clear parts, and precision scribing tools.

### 6. Tamiya

- **Type:** Tools & Supplies
- **Description:** An indispensable name in the modeling world. While famous for many products, their "Tamiya Panel Line Accent Color" and "Tamiya Extra Thin Cement" are essential items found on almost every modeler's workbench.

# II. CATEGORIES

### 1. Plastic Model Kits

- **Description:** The core of the hobby, where builders cut parts from runners and assemble them. Includes authentic Bandai kits and third-party mecha with die-cast frames.

### 2. Cutting & Finishing Tools

- **Description:** Focuses on removing parts and cleaning nub marks to ensure the model looks its best. Includes single-edged nippers, glass files, and sanding sets.

### 3. Painting & Panel Lining

- **Description:** Designed for adding depth and color, making mechanical details look realistic. Includes Gundam Markers, Panel Line accents, Lacquer/Acrylic paints, and Topcoats.

### 4. Detail-up Parts & Accessories

- **Description:** Products used to upgrade the look or add features to the original kit. Includes water-slide decals, weapon sets, LED units, metal parts, and cutting mats.

# III. SERIES

### 1. Mobile Suit Gundam (Universal Century - UC)

- **Description:** The original series established in 1979. It centers on the conflict between the Earth Federation and the Principality of Zeon. Sequels like _Zeta Gundam_ (40th Anniversary in 2025) and _Gundam Unicorn_ remain monuments of deep political and military storytelling.

### 2. Mobile Suit Gundam SEED / SEED Freedom

- **Description:** The most commercially successful alternate universe series. Known for beautiful robot designs and dramatic characters, it recently surged in popularity again in 2024-2025 thanks to the record-breaking movie "Gundam SEED Freedom".

### 3. Mobile Suit Gundam: The Witch from Mercury

- **Description:** The biggest TV series in recent years (2022). Featuring the first female protagonist and a school setting mixed with corporate warfare, it successfully attracted a new, younger generation of fans to the hobby.

### 4. Mobile Suit Gundam: Iron-Blooded Orphans (IBO)

- **Description:** Celebrating its 10th anniversary in 2025. Famous for its raw, pragmatic combat style (no beam weapons) and a tragic story of orphans fighting to survive. IBO kits (like Barbatos) are consistently best-sellers.

### 5. Mobile Suit Gundam 00

- **Description:** Set in the near future involving solar energy and realistic political conflicts. The Gundam designs here are modern and sleek, highly favored by modelers who love sharp angles and advanced technology.

### 6. Gundam Build Series (Fighters / Divers / Metaverse)

- **Description:** A spin-off dedicated to Gunpla culture. Instead of war, characters build models and battle in virtual simulations. This series strongly promotes creativity and customization within the community.

### 7. Mobile Suit Gundam: Requiem for Vengeance (2024-2025)

- **Description:** A Netflix original series using Unreal Engine 5 technology. Set on the European Front during the One Year War from Zeon's perspective. The kits feature a realistic, military industrial design, perfect for fans of weathering and gritty aesthetics.
